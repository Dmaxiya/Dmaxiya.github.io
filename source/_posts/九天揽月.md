## <center>菩萨蛮</center>

### <center>九天揽月</center>

<center>清风拂月潭中映，</center>

<center>误咫尺水浮虚境。</center>

<center>游子渺风尘，</center>

<center>忧思多少人。</center>

<br/>

<center>婵娟愁几许，</center>

<center>揽物盍环玉？</center>

<center>古万户飞天，</center>

<center>今华夏傲颜！</center>
